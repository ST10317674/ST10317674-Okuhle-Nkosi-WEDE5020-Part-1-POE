MIET AFRICA Film on Climate Change Scoops Film Award

We are thrilled to announce that our short film, 'Our Changing Climate - Episode 1', was named Best Conservation/Wildlife Documentary Short at the prestigious 10th Annual Simon 'Mabhunu' Sabela KZN Film & Television Awards! This award recognizes our innovative two-episode television series, 'Our Changing Climate - Our Time to Act', which highlights the inspiring efforts of young climate activists in adapting to and mitigating climate change. The series depicts a future shaped by these proactive measures, highlighting a promising path to climate resilience. The TV series was produced by Tekwini Productions as part of the SADC FutureLife-Now! Programme, in collaboration with UNITAR and the UN CC: Learn programme. It was supplemented by a 12-episode radio series that aired in Malawi, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

FutureLife-Now! Showcased at International Trade Fair

FutureLife-Now! remains unwavering in its commitment to empowering Zambia's youth and creating a brighter, healthier future for all. FutureLife-Now! showed off its transformative work at the prestigious 2023 International Trade Fair (ITF), emphasizing its commitment to youth empowerment and wellness. This high-profile event, held from June 26 to July 2, 2023, under the theme "Stimulating Economic Development Through Partnerships, Trade, and Investment," provided an opportunity for networking and forging new partnerships. The program's participation highlighted its critical role in addressing health and wellness issues among Zambia's young people. FutureLife-Now! proudly showcased its strategic collaboration with the Zambian Ministry of Health, demonstrating their shared efforts to improve adolescent healthcare. This partnership exemplifies the program's commitment to integrating health and empowerment initiatives, paving the way for a healthier generation and establishing a precedent for effective public-private partnerships.
